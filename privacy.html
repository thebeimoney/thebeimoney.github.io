Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 16th April 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Bravo Echo Indi Services Limited ("we," "our," or "us"). We are an operator based in Hong Kong SAR, China, providing services through our mobile application and website (collectively, our "Services"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal data in compliance with the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) of Hong Kong and other applicable laws and regulations.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our Services. By downloading our application, visiting our website, registering for an account, or using our Services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Beyond the Privacy Policy, your use of our Services is also subject to our Terms of Service.

Information We Collect

We may collect a variety of information from or about you or your devices from various sources, as described below. If you do not provide your information when requested, you may not be able to use our Services if that information is necessary to provide you with our Services or if we are legally required to collect it.

Information You Provide to Us

Registration and Profile Information: During registration, we collect your name, date of birth, email address, phone number, and location. This information is required to create a restricted account and facilitate the identity verification process.

When you register to use our Services, we ask you for:

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Details in identity documents (e.g. Hong Kong Identity Card, passport)
  • Contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Residential address and proof of address
  • Photographs and other verification data

Biometric Data: During onboarding and identity verification, we collect biometric data which includes:

  • Facial geometry data from your selfie
  • Photo of your government-issued ID

This data is used to verify your identity and prevent fraud. By agreeing to our collection of your biometric data, you consent to its collection, use, sharing, and retention as described in this Privacy Policy.

Communications: If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you, such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of a message or attachments that you may send to us, and other information you choose to provide. When we send you emails, we may track whether you open them to learn how to deliver a better customer experience and improve our Services.

Careers: If you decide to apply for a job with us, you may submit your contact information and your resume online. We will collect the information you choose to provide on your resume, such as your education and employment experience.

Payment Information: If you make a transaction through our Services, your payment-related information, such as debit card number, bank account numbers, or other financial information, is collected by us or our third-party payment processor on our behalf.

Information We Collect When You Use Our Services

Location Information: We infer your general location information, for example by using your internet protocol (IP) address.

Device Information: We receive information about the device and software you use to access our Services, including IP address, web browser type, operating system version, phone carrier and manufacturer, application installations, device identifiers, mobile advertising identifiers, and push notification tokens.

Usage Information: To help us understand how you use our Services and to help us improve them, we automatically receive information about your interactions with our Services, like the pages or other content you view, transactions you enter into, and other information related to your usage of the Services.

Your Contacts: If you permit us to access the address book on your device or give us permission to import your contacts from an email account associated with your account, we will access and store names and contact information from your address book to facilitate interactions that you initiate through our Services.

Information from Cookies and Similar Technologies: We and our third-party partners may collect information using cookies, pixel tags, and similar technologies on our website and mobile application. These technologies help us analyze how users navigate our Services, personalize your experience, and facilitate the functionality of our Services.

Information We Receive from Third Parties

Identity Verification Services: We may receive information about you from our identity verification partners to verify your identity and comply with regulatory requirements.

Other Third Parties: We may receive additional information about you from third parties such as data or marketing partners and combine it with information we have about you. We may receive information about you from our partners in connection with fraud prevention, identity verification, and other purposes related to legal compliance.

How We Use the Information We Collect

The information collected during registration is used to:

  • Create a restricted account to provide limited app access
  • Enable communication with you regarding completing the identity verification process
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements for account creation and restricted access

We use the information we collect:

  • To provide, maintain, improve, and enhance our Services
  • To verify your personal information to allow access to additional functionality of our products and Services
  • To understand and analyze how you use our Services and develop new products, services, features, and functionality
  • To communicate with you, provide you with updates and other information relating to our Services, provide information that you request, respond to comments and questions, and otherwise provide customer support
  • To send you service-related notifications
  • To facilitate transactions and payments
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, violations of our terms or policies, and other harmful or illegal activity
  • To comply with legal obligations, including anti-money laundering and counter-terrorist financing requirements
  • To protect the rights, property, and safety of you, us, and others
  • For other purposes for which we provide specific notice at the time the information is collected

How We Use and Share Biometric Data

The biometric data you provide during onboarding is used to:

  • Grant you restricted access to the app while ensuring that financial services remain disabled until your identity is verified
  • Verify your identity securely by comparing the facial geometry extracted from your ID and selfie
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized account access

We share your identity information and selfie with our third-party service provider who can compare the face geometry extracted from your ID and selfie to securely verify your identity.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal grounds:

  • For the performance of our contract with you
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations
  • For our legitimate business interests, such as fraud prevention and service improvement
  • Based on your consent, where applicable

We do not disclose information we collect from or about you except as described below or otherwise disclosed to you at the time of the collection.

Regulatory Bodies and Law Enforcement:

As Required by Law: We may access, preserve, and disclose your information if we believe doing so is required or appropriate to: (a) comply with legal obligations; (b) respond to your requests; or (c) protect your, our, or others' rights, property, or safety.

Your Choices

Account Information: You may update, correct, or delete your account information at any time by accessing your account settings in our application or on our website. Note that we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Deletion Request: You may request the deletion of your registered data if you choose not to proceed with identity verification. Please note that deletion of this data may result in losing access to restricted account features.

Marketing Communications: You can opt out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in those communications. You may still receive service-related communications.

Cookies: You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you disable or refuse cookies, please note that some parts of our Services may be inaccessible or not function properly.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our Services to you
  • Comply with our legal and regulatory obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

Under the Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorist Financing Ordinance (Cap. 615), we are required to keep records of customer identification and transactions for at least 5 years after the end of the business relationship or completion of the transaction.

For users who do not complete the identity verification process, registered data (including name, date of birth, email, phone, and any biometric data) is retained for a maximum of three (3) years. After this period, the data will be securely deleted unless required by law or regulatory obligations.

Retention of Biometric Data

  • Our service provider maintains a copy of your identity document and selfie for identity verification purposes and to comply with our legal record keeping requirements for up to five (5) years from your account closure.
  • Our service provider deletes your information within five (5) years at maximum from your last interaction with them.

Destruction of Biometric Data

Our service providers and partners permanently erase your biometric data at the end of the applicable retention period described above such that it cannot be recovered or reconstructed.

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Staff training on data protection
  • Secure data storage systems

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
